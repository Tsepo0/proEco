Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Pro Eco-Connect

Signing up is easy! Visit our Farmers page and click "Sign Up as Farmer". You'll need to provide basic information about your farm, products, and location. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 2 business days.

For farmers, we charge a small commission only when you make a sale (typically 5-10%, depending on your subscription plan). Buyers can browse for free and only pay for the products they purchase. No hidden fees!

Delivery arrangements are made directly between farmers and buyers. Many farmers offer delivery within a certain radius, while others may require pickup. Our platform helps coordinate logistics but doesn't handle delivery directly.

Absolutely! Many of our buyers set up standing orders with their favorite farmers. You can specify delivery frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, etc.) and easily manage your orders through your account dashboard.

We use secure payment processing with encryption for all transactions. Our platform also includes farmer verification, buyer reviews, and dispute resolution services to ensure safe and fair transactions for all parties.
