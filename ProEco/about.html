Our Story

From humble beginnings to revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain

2018 - The Idea

Our founder, a third-generation farmer, recognized the inefficiencies in the agricultural supply chain while struggling to get fair prices for his organic produce.

2019 - First Prototype

Developed the initial platform connecting 12 local farmers with 50 consumers in our hometown, reducing food waste by 30% and increasing farmer profits by 45%.

2020 - Official Launch

Expanded to three states, serving over 1,000 customers and 200 farmers during the pandemic when traditional supply chains were disrupted.

2022 - National Expansion

Implemented our AI-powered logistics system, allowing us to serve 15 states with over 10,000 active users and 2,500 farming partners.
